About Top Edu

Top Edu is an Auckland-based learning centre dedicated to helping students build strong academic foundations, confident thinking skills, and long-term learning ability.

We specialise in Cambridge and international curriculum support, providing structured guidance in Mathematics, Science, English and critical thinking. Our programmes are designed not only to improve grades, but to develop independent learners who understand concepts deeply and can apply knowledge in real situations.

More Detail

OUR LEADER: Kevin Li

Kevin Li is the founder of Top Edu and has been deeply involved in international education and academic training for many years. With a background in data analytics and a strong interest in learning psychology, he focuses on building structured learning systems that help students develop clear thinking and independent problem-solving ability.

Kevin believes that true academic improvement does not come from short-term memorisation, but from understanding the logic behind knowledge. He has designed teaching frameworks that emphasise step-by-step reasoning, concept clarity and consistent practice, allowing students to steadily improve while gaining confidence in their learning.

Over the years, Kevin has worked closely with families from diverse educational backgrounds and understands the challenges students face when transitioning between different curricula and learning environments. His goal is to make learning more efficient, less stressful and more meaningful for each student.

At Top Edu, Kevin leads the development of curriculum structure, teaching methodology and long-term learning pathways, ensuring that students not only perform well in examinations but also build the ability to learn independently in the future.

His educational philosophy: Help students understand, not memorise — and once they understand, progress naturally follows.

MAIN COURSES

Choose from our wide range of expertly designed courses tailored to help students excel in their academic journey.

English Language

Our English programme strengthens reading comprehension, writing structure, grammar, and vocabulary. Students gain practical skills to perform better in classroom learning and assessments.

Mathematics

We build strong foundations in core maths concepts and problem-solving skills. Step-by-step guidance helps students improve accuracy, confidence, and logical thinking for school success.

Chinese Language

We support literacy development through reading practice and structured writing. Students improve comprehension, language expression, and confidence in academic Chinese.

Science

We simplify key scientific concepts and connect learning to real-life examples. Students develop curiosity, understanding, and confidence in school science subjects.

Physics

We teach physics concepts step by step, making complex ideas easier to understand. Students strengthen problem-solving skills and apply formulas with confidence.

Chemistry

Our chemistry classes clarify core principles through structured teaching and practice. Students learn to apply concepts accurately in calculations and exam questions.

Biology

Our biology lessons focus on clear explanations of life processes and systems. Students build strong understanding of key topics and prepare effectively for tests and exams.
